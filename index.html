FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite as many team members as your current plan allows. Check your billing settings for details.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" at the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings panel.

Do you provide additional support?

Yes, our support team is available 24/7 via live chat and email support.
